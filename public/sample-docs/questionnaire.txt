# Security Questionnaire - CloudTrack

## Data Protection Questions

1. Do you encrypt data at rest?
2. What encryption standard do you use?
3. Where are your servers located?
4. Is multi-factor authentication required for employees?

## Access Control Questions

5. Do you have role-based access control implemented?
6. What are the different access levels in your organization?
7. Are access logs maintained and reviewed?

## Backup & Disaster Recovery

8. Are daily automated backups performed?
9. What is your backup retention policy?
10. What is your disaster recovery plan and RTO?

## Security Practices

11. Do you perform regular security audits?
12. Are employees trained in security practices?
13. What is your data retention and deletion policy?

## Compliance

14. Are you compliant with GDPR?
15. What industry standards or certifications do you have?
