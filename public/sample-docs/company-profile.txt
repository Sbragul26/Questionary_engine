# CloudTrack - Company Profile

**Company Name:** CloudTrack

**Industry:** SaaS (Cloud Software)

**Description:** CloudTrack is a SaaS company that provides project management software for small businesses. It stores customer data in the cloud and follows strict security practices.

## Company Overview
CloudTrack has been operating since 2020 and serves over 5,000 small to medium-sized businesses globally. The company specializes in providing intuitive project management tools with advanced collaboration features.

## Mission
To empower small businesses with affordable, secure, and easy-to-use project management tools that help teams collaborate effectively.
