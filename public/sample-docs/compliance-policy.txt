# CloudTrack - Compliance & Audit Policy

## Security Audits

Annual third-party security audits are performed by reputable cybersecurity firms. Audit reports are available upon request from prospective customers.

## Employee Training

All employees receive mandatory security training during onboarding. Quarterly security awareness training is conducted to keep employees updated on latest threats and best practices.

Topics covered in security training:
- Phishing and social engineering awareness
- Password management and security
- Data classification and handling
- Incident reporting procedures
- Compliance requirements

## Data Retention

Customer data is retained as long as the customer maintains an active subscription. Upon account deletion, customer data is securely deleted within 30 days.

Data deletion process:
1. Data is flagged for deletion
2. 30-day grace period for recovery requests
3. Cryptographic deletion using AES-256 key destruction
4. Deletion verification logs are generated

## Compliance Standards

CloudTrack is compliant with:
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- SOC 2 Type II certification in progress
- ISO 27001 certification in progress

## Incident Response

We maintain a 24/7 security incident response team. All incidents are logged and investigated. Customer notification procedures follow regulatory requirements.
